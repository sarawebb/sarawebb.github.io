About Me

I am currently a PhD student with the Centre of Astrophysics and Supercomputing at Swinburne University. When I am not researching I will be out patting the wonderful dogs I meet around the Hawthorn area, or getting crafty at our 'Arty Astro' nights.

Research

I am currently working as part of the Deeper, Wider, Faster Program looking for the fastest explosions in our Universe. Our program, DWF, consists of over 50 telescope facilities world wide spanning the entire electromagnetic spectrum. We hope to explore a region of parametre space never before explored with our high cadence and simultaneous multiwavelength observations. My GitHub has repos of my work and past research.

Outreach
